Kenlee's Scone Recipe

Indulge in the comforting aroma of freshly baked scones with this easy and delightful recipe. These classic buttery scones are the perfect accompaniment to your morning tea or a cozy afternoon treat. With a crisp exterior giving way to a tender, flaky interior, each bite is a symphony of flavors that will transport you to a quaint English tearoom.


These are the ingredients that you will need to make these beautiful scones

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, cold and cut into small pieces
  • 2/3 cup milk (whole milk or buttermilk)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Scones Steps!

  1. Preheat & Prep:

      Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. Dry Ingredients:

    <
      In a large bowl, whisk together 2 cups of all-purpose flour, 1/4 cup sugar, 1 tbsp baking powder, and 1/2 tsp salt.

  3. Butter and Mix-Ins:

      Add 1/2 cup cold, chopped butter to the dry mix. Optional: Fold in 1/2 cup raisins, currants, or chocolate chips.

  4. Wet Ingredients:

      In another bowl, mix 2/3 cup milk and 1 tsp vanilla extract.

  5. Combine and Shape:

      Pour the wet mix into the dry mix and stir until just combined. Turn out the dough onto a floured surface, gently knead, and shape into a 1-inch thick circle.

  6. Cut and Arrange:

      Use a round cutter or glass to cut out scones and place them on the prepared baking sheet.

  7. Baking Time!:

      Bake for 12-15 minutes until the scones are golden brown.

  8. Cool and Enjoy!:

      Allow the scones to cool briefly on a wire rack and serve them warm with clotted cream, jam, or butter.
