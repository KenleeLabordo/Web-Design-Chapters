Overview

"Rogue One: A Star Wars Story" is a standalone film in the Star Wars franchise that was released in 2016. Directed by Gareth Edwards, the movie is set just before the events of the original 1977 film, "Star Wars: Episode IV - A New Hope." Below is an overview of the plot, key information, cast, characters,and some reviews:

Plot

The story follows a group of rebels who embark on a dangerous mission to steal the plans for the Death Star, the Galactic Empire's superweapon capable of destroying entire planets.
